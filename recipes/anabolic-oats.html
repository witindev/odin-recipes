Anabolic-Oats

Anabolic-Oats dish that's not only delicious but healthy too

This recipe gives a new meaning to eating. It's protein-packed nutrient contents ensure that you are giving your body what it needs, and to your mind the food cravings it wants!

With the perfect blend from what's sweet and salty, this oat-meal recipe will never be boring.

Ingredients

  • Rolled Oats
  • Water
  • Cow Milk (of your choice)
  • Whey Protein-Powder (chocolate or vanilla recommended)
  • Apple
  • Ground Cinnamon

Recipe Instructions

  1. Add 60g of Rolled-Oats into a bowl.
  2. Pour 240ml of water (or 4x the Oats quantity).
  3. Put the bowl into the microwave for 1:30 minutes.
  4. Take the bowl out and put 30g of whey protein and 30ml of milk on top.
  5. Mix the Oats and the new contents.
  6. Cut an apple into bite-sized pieces and mix with 1g of cinnamon in another bowl.
  7. Put the new bowl into the microwave for 1:00 minute.
  8. Add the apple contents on top of the Anabolic-Oats.
  9. Last but not least, Enjoy!
