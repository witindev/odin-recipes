Protein Ice-Cream

Ice-Cream as healthy, cheap, and delicious has never been seen

This protein ice-cream with berry flavour is sure to make you addicted to it.

Packed with protein and good flavour, what makes this ice-cream better than others is the simple ingredient list and low calorie count, which makes it easy to make, cheap, and a success.

Ingredients

  • Protein Powder (vanilla whey being chef's choice)
  • Almond Milk (unsweetened)
  • Frozen berries mix
  • Xanthan Gum

Recipe Instructions

  1. Add protein powder, almond milk, xanthan gum, and frozen berries to a blender and mix until well combined
  2. Place in a container and chill in the freezer for at least 3 hours
  3. Thaw for a few minutes and enjoy!
