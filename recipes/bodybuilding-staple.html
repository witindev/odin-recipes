Bodybuilding Staple Meal

The "bro" diet, a way for bodybuilders to become themselves

There is nothing as good as the bodybuilding staple of rice, chicken, and broccoli when on a diet to achieve your dream physique.

Perfect combination of carbs, proteins, and vitamins that will aid is muscle recovery and let you be in a deficit without suffering for calories every meal.

Ingredients

  • Rice (Jasmine being chef's choice)
  • Chicken Breasts
  • Broccoli
  • Salt
  • Ground Pepper

Recipe Instructions

  1. Get out your pot and include 100g of rice with 250ml (2.5 times the rice quantity), and let it burn on high
  2. As the rice starts to boil in the pot, lower the temperature to low, and put a lid on
  3. Let the cook rice for 15-20 minutes, stir and then serve at the end
  4. Cut the broccoli from its stems into bite-sized pieces, in total approx 250g
  5. Cook the broccoli on a frying pan on high heat until crispy
  6. Get a chicken thigh (200g) and cut it sideways for a thinner cut
  7. Season the chicken on both sides with salt and pepper (feel free to add other spices!)
  8. Add olive oil onto the frying pan and cook on high till cooked through
  9. Place all the ingredients within the same platter and enjoy!
